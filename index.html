Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, products, or methods that improve upon existing solutions.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting current needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the foundational systems and structures—like transportation, energy, and communication support the functioning of society.

Empowerment

Empowerment is about giving individuals the tools, resources, and confidence to take control of their own lives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Technology is a powerful tool, but its true value lies in how we use it. While it has the potential to solve problems and improve lives, its impact depends on our intentions and actions. In fields like healthcare, communication, and sustainability, technology can drive positive change when applied responsibly. However, without careful consideration, it can also create unintended consequences. To ensure that technology benefits society, we must use it ethically, focusing on promoting equity, well-being, and sustainability. Our choices shape its role in the future.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
